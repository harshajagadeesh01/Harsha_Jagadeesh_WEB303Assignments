Elon Musk

Biography

Elon Musk is a business magnate, industrial designer, and engineer. Born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa, he is the CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, among other ventures.

Tesla

Tesla, Inc. is an electric vehicle and clean energy company founded by Elon Musk. It manufactures electric cars, solar products, and energy storage solutions.

SpaceX

SpaceX is a private aerospace manufacturer and space transportation company founded by Elon Musk. It focuses on reducing space transportation costs and enabling the colonization of Mars.
